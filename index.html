About the Artist

Lynn M. Ruiz

Lynn M. Ruiz is a mosaic artist based in the Pacific Northwest. Working primarily in glass, ceramic, and stone tesserae, she creates richly textured works that explore the interplay of color, light, and form. Her studio practice spans over two decades, informed by a lifelong fascination with the way small, singular pieces can come together to form something wholly unified.

Her work has been shown in regional galleries and private collections throughout the United States. She accepts a limited number of commissions each year.

Artist Statement

On Making

I am drawn to the fragment — the single tessera, the broken edge, the piece that seems incomplete on its own. Mosaic is the art of patient accumulation: trust the process long enough and the image emerges, whole and luminous, from what looked like chaos.

My subjects are drawn from the natural world — light on water, the geometry of bark and leaf, the way color shifts at dusk. I am interested in how we perceive wholeness from proximity and distance both.
